The CS Scholars Program at UC Berkeley offers a "home" within Cal for diverse undeclared Letters and Science students interested in the computer science major. We aim to support students from underserved backgrounds who might not have had access to resources that prepare students to succeed in computer science.

We seek passionate, creative students who wish to give back to the community and to each other.
